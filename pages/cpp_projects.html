Making Games
The form works, mostly. If you put your name in and click submit you'll get a popup window using the input fields. I'm not really sure what else I should write here for now, but maybe this'll do. Have you ever made a video game before? If it's something that you think you'd like to try, I encourage it. I love to program, and making games is highly enjoyable to me. I know some would say that I'm crazy for putting myself through the anguish of sitting for hours staring at a computer writing code. I will admit that we all have to be some kind of crazy for this type of work to be something that we actually want to do. The reward for me however is when I get to see someone play my game, and enjoy it. I've had a few people test games there we at most playable. To see them laugh and have a good time playing them made all the hours of programming and debugging worth it. Bringing joy to people's lives is something I truly enjoy, and this happens to be the way that I do that. Making games is fun. I enjoy making them. Don't ask me why, but I do. The satisfaction of seeing people play my games makes me happy.
