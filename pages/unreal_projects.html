Unreal Engine 4 Projects

This assignment was one I did over a course of a quarter while attending Southern New Hampshire University - Online. It's done all in Unreal's C++ API, and was a very fun project. I call it "Assignment Mash-Up" because it was a bunch of assignements all mashed into one game project.

"Clean Your Room" started off as a school assignment, but I enjoyed the concept so much that I continued development. It's still under development to this day, but it has been converted to a Unity3D project.

Ravvit was an idea I had while watching a co-worker draw Odie from "Garfield and Friends" on a white board. It looked nothing like Odie at first, but instead looked like the little guy in the picture. Ravvit is a 2D character drawn in Adobe Illustrator and exported as a 2D sprite sheet into Spriter to do the 2D animation. From there I used Unreal to program the movement and used Unreal's Flipbook for the animation blending.
